About Ayacucho

Our Place of Travel

Ayacucho is characterized by a temperate and dry climate, with solar brightness all year round. The city has both national and international fame thanks to its handicraft pieces, which is why it was declared the "Capital of Popular Art and Crafts of Peru". It is a city of very high Catholic fervor. It has more than thirty colonial temples of Renaissance, Baroque and Mestizo style, in addition, you can see colonial mansions, archaeological remains and artistic manifestations that reveal a historical past and a still current and attractive tradition. It also stands out for its music and festivities, such as carnivals and especially Holy Week, both declared Cultural Patrimony of the Nation. This last one is considered as the second most important in the world, as far as the celebration of Holy Week is concerned.
